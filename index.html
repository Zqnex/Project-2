Virginia and the 2020 Election

How to Use:

Just press the 'Take me Somewhere' button to go to a different county. Then press 'Show the Results' to see how many voted for the Republican or Democrat canidate.

Artist Statement

This project was started before the current election happened so kind of being in the spirit of our future government I wanted to make a little showing of our state, Virginia, and some of the counties and how they voted along with the numbers. I don't know why, but this year I paid attention and such way more than I ever have before towards the election.
